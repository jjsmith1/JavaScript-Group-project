  1. The first player chooses a card and carefully turns it over.
  2. The player then selects another card and turns it over.
  3. If the two cards are a matching pair for example two yellows then the two cards will stay visible
  4. The first player gets another turn.
  5. If the cards are not a match they are turned back over and it is now player twos turn.
  6. A players turn is not over until they are unable to make a matching pair.
